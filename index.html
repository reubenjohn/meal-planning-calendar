😴 Lazy Day Recipes

Quick backup meals requiring minimal or no cooking - perfect for busy days!

🥚 Boiled Egg + Avocado Bowl

⏱️ 5 min prep

Use pre-boiled eggs (made in weekend batches). Slice eggs and avocado, serve over salad mix with olive oil, lemon juice, and pepper.

🍓 Yogurt + Fruit Bowl

⏱️ 2 min prep

Add sliced banana or berries to plain yogurt. Optional: drizzle honey or sprinkle granola for extra flavor.

🫓 Paneer Wrap (Lazy)

⏱️ 5 min prep

Use leftover paneer tikka masala as filling. Wrap with salad mix or sautéed onions in whole wheat tortilla.

🫓 Fish Wrap

⏱️ 5 min prep

Reheat leftover air-fried fish. Wrap in tortilla with salad mix and yogurt/lemon drizzle. Assemble in under 5 minutes.

🍛 Shrimp Rice Bowl

⏱️ 5 min prep

Use leftover shrimp curry served over rice or salad mix. Optional: top with boiled egg or yogurt drizzle.

🥗 Chicken Salad Bowl

⏱️ 5 min prep

Use roasted chicken from Saturday + salad mix. Optional toppings: boiled egg, avocado, lemon/olive oil dressing.

🥙 Chicken Wrap

⏱️ 5 min prep

Use leftover roasted chicken. Wrap with salad mix in tortilla + yogurt drizzle or leftover dal curry as a base.

🥚 Simple Boiled Eggs

⏱️ 2 min prep

Slice pre-boiled eggs. Serve with salt, pepper, optional toast or fruit. Uses eggs boiled earlier in the week.
