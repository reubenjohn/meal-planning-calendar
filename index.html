😴 Lazy Day Recipes

Quick & easy meals requiring minimal or no cooking - perfect for busy days!

🥚 Boiled Eggs

⏱️ 0-5 min prep

Hard-boil eggs in advance (Sunday or Tuesday). Serve with salt, pepper, and a side of avocado or salad mix.

🍓 Yogurt + Fruit

⏱️ 2 min prep

Greek yogurt with fresh berries, banana slices, or any seasonal fruit. Add a drizzle of honey if desired.

🥗 Salad Bowl

⏱️ 3-5 min prep

Store-bought salad mix with leftover proteins (chicken, paneer), cherry tomatoes, cucumber, and simple dressing.

🫓 Quick Wraps

⏱️ 3-5 min prep

Whole wheat wraps filled with leftover proteins, salad mix, and sauces. Roll up and eat!

🍛 Rice Bowls

⏱️ 2-3 min prep

Pre-cooked rice (made in bulk) topped with leftover curries, shrimp, or dal. Microwave if needed.

🥚 Egg Bowl

⏱️ 5 min prep

Scrambled or boiled eggs over rice or with toast. Add soy sauce, sriracha, or simple seasonings.

🥑 Avocado + Eggs

⏱️ 3 min prep

Sliced avocado with boiled eggs, salt, pepper, and a squeeze of lemon. Eat with toast or alone.

🍲 Leftover Curries

⏱️ 2-3 min reheat

Dal, paneer curry, or any leftover curry from cook days. Serve with rice or eat as-is.
